The HP DeskJet 2855e All-in-One Wireless Printer

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐


9.5 Printer Assistance


The HP DeskJet 2855e is a compact, budget-friendly wireless printer designed for home use. With its all-in-one functionality—print, scan, and copy—it is ideal for occasional printing of documents, homework, and color prints. Its simple design, easy setup, and wireless connectivity make it a popular choice among casual users.


Key Features


All-in-One Function: Print, scan, and copy capabilities in a single device.

Print Speed: Up to 7.5 ppm (black) and 5.5 ppm (color).

Print Resolution: Up to 1200 x 1200 dpi (black), up to 4800 x 1200 optimized dpi (color).

Wireless Connectivity: Built-in Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz), Apple AirPrint, Mopria, and HP Smart App support.

Paper Handling: 60-sheet input tray, 25-sheet output tray, manual duplex printing.

Ink Compatibility: Uses HP 67 and HP 67XL ink cartridges (black & tri-color).

Display: Icon LCD with physical buttons for basic operations.

Scanner: Flatbed scanner with up to 1200 dpi optical resolution.

Compact Design: Lightweight and space-saving, ideal for small desks or shelves.

HP+ Ready: Includes 3 months of free Instant Ink subscription with HP+ activation.



The HP DeskJet 2855e is a true all-in-one printer, combining printing, scanning, and copying functions in a single compact device. Whether you’re printing school assignments, scanning important documents, or making quick copies of receipts, this printer offers versatile functionality suited for everyday use at home.


With built-in Wi-Fi connectivity, this printer eliminates the need for cumbersome cables. It supports Apple AirPrint, Mopria Print Service, and the HP Smart App, allowing users to print and scan directly from smartphones, tablets, or laptops. This means you can print from anywhere in your home—perfect for modern, mobile lifestyles.



The DeskJet 2855e has a sleek and compact build that fits easily on desks, shelves, or small tables. With its minimal footprint and lightweight body (just 3.42 kg), it's a great choice for students or home users working with limited space.


Setting up the printer is straightforward with the HP Smart AppThe app walks you through each step, from connecting to Wi-Fi to loading ink cartridges. The front control panel features an icon-based LCD display and tactile buttons, making basic operations like copy or cancel easy and intuitive—even for beginners.
